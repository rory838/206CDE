About Open Export

Different countries have different product standards. Rules, regulations, requirements, red tape.Open Export provides an automated service to ensure your products are ready for export to any country in the world. If you let us know where you would like to export to, and provide details of the products you intend to export, we can provide a complete service by securing all relevant export technical requirements to allow you to sell your products anywhere in the world. Open Export is a one stop shop for compliance with foreign regulatory protocols. Trade anywhere with Open Export.

Our ultimate goal is to enable

any manufacturer, in any country, to overcome any technical standard, in order to export any equipment, anywhere.
